The International Baccalaureate® (IB)

The International Baccalaureate® (IB) Programme offers high quality programmes of international education to a worldwide community of schools. There are more than 900,000 IB students in over 140 countries.

 

Marymount Academy International has been an IB World School since February 2001. It offers the IB Middle Years Programme to 440 students.

The MYP is designed for students aged 11 to 16. It provides a framework of learning which encourages students to become creative, critical and reflective thinkers. Read More...
